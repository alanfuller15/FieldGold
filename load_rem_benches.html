Load REM Terrace Candidates

This adds 20 REM-derived bench candidates to your FieldGold shared record, alongside your existing Bench Hunter benches.

Honest about what these are: geomorphically-derived terrace candidates — flat ground at bench height above the real river, extracted by the published REM / TerEx method from lidar. They are NOT confirmed benches and NOT gold. The Little Su's gold is likely morainal (glacial), which a REM can't distinguish from sorted fluvial terraces. These are first-pass targets to go pan — the ground makes the call.

Ranked by area + height + documented-gold proximity (geochem is sparse on this reach, so ranking is mostly area + height — see each candidate's gold m).

This augments — it won't remove your Bench Hunter benches. REM benches are tagged source: REM and labeled REM-1..N so they're distinct. To remove them later, use the database tools in the main app.
